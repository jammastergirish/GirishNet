Or you can just use direct links. For instance, if you wanted to search for the keyword "javascript", then you would link to index.htm?javascript . Or search for "javascript" or "perl": index.htm?javascript%20perl&or. Or for "javascript" and "perl": index.htm?javascript%20perl&and. Or for the exact phrase "javascript perl": index.htm?javascript%20perl&exact. (note that %20 is the URL escaped form of a space).
