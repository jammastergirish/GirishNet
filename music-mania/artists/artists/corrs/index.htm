The Corrs are Andrea Corr (Lead Vocals/Tin Whistle), Sharon Corr (Background Vocals/Violin), Caroline Corr (Background Vocals/Drums) and Jim Corr (Guitars/Keyboards/Programming). When the Irish band asked American house DJ Todd Terry (had a hit with Something's Going On) to remix their cover of Fleetwood Mac's Dreams, fans must of thought they were mad, but the song went into the Top 10 in the UK, this was the bands first major hit. Their next hits included Runaway (which was re-released), What Can I Do? and So Young.
Talk On Corners managed to get into the UK Top 10, a few weeks after its release, they released the 'Special Edition' version (blue cover) with one extra track and some new remixes.
