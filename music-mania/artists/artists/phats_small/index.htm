Phats & Small

NOW Phats What I
Small Music
[BUY] [INFO/REVIEW]

Jason Phats, Russel Small and vocalist 'Big' Ben's first hit in December 1998, Turn Around. It was Fatboy Slim who spotted the songs potential, he was playing it in clubs long before its release in December. Turn Around peaked at number 2, and hung around in the top 10 for nearly two months. Feel Good, their next single came out at the end of July. An album will be released in October 1999, NOW Phats What I Small Music - Volume One!
