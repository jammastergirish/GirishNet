Time Warner Joins Forces With EMI

EMI announced today that they will merge its recording and publishing businesses with the Warner Music Group, part of US giant Time Warner. EMI's artists include the Beatles, Robbie Williams, Radiohead and the Spice Girls. Warner's artists include The Corrs, Cher, Madonna, Catatonia and Simply Red.
A couple of weeks ago Time Warner announced a merger with internet provider AOL, this will mean that EMI can sell more music online.
