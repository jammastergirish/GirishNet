Comments About Oasis's New LP

Recently NME gave some stars a sneak preview of Oasis's new album, Standing on the Shoulders of Giants. Here's what they had to say:

Bryan McFadden, Westlife
"It's a rip off of the Beatles later stuff. The single's just a rip-off of 'Strawberry Fields'." "His voice (Liam's) is wrecked as well."
"Jesus, when I first heard the single I was nearly sick!" How did you feel when you first heard Westlife?, Bryan goes on, "It's taking the piss. I'm embarassed by it."

Jamie Theakston
"There's alot of records I'm looking forward to this year, like Richard Ashcroft and Radiohead, but I have to say I was never that excited about this and it hasn't done much to prove me wrong." "It sounds very over-produced and it might be better if there was less going on. The ones Noel sings are okay but, again, they'll sound better when he plays them by himself live with just an acoustic guitar."
