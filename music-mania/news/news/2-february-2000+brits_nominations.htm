Brit Awards Nominations

So the Brit Awards 2000 are to be hosted by Divina McCall on March 3rd, here are the nominations:

Best British Group
Blur; Gomez; Stereophonics; Texas; Travis

Mastercard Best British Album
Basement Jaxx - Remedy; The Chemical Brothers -Surrender; Gomez - Liquid Skin; Stereophonics - Performance & Cocktails; Travis - The Man Who

Best British Newcomer
Adam Rickitt; Ann Lee; Beta Band; Blockster; Charlotte Church; Death In Vegas; Fierce; Gay Dad; Glamma Kid; Groove Armada; Honeyz; Jamelia; Kele Le Roc; Martine McCutcheon; Phats & Small; S Club 7; Shanks & Bigfoot; Spacedust, UNKLE; Wiseguys

Best British Dance Act
Basement Jaxx; The Chemical Brother; Fatboy Slim; Jamiroquai; Leftfield

Best Soundtrack/Cast Recording
Austin Powers (The Spy Who Shagged Me); Notting Hill; Star Wars (Episode One: The Phantom Menace); Fight Club; Music by The Dust Brothers; The Matrix

Best International Group
Beastie Boys; The Cardigans; Mercury Rev; Red Hot Chilli Peppers; TLC

Best International Male Solo Artist
Beck; Eminem; Ricky Martin; Will Smith

Best International Female Solo Artist
Britney Spears; Jennifer Lopez; Macy Gray; Mary J Blige; Whitney Houston

Best British Male Solo Artist
David Bowie; Ian Brown; Sting; Tom Jones; Van Morrison

Best British Female Solo Artist
Beth Orton; Beverly Knight; Gabrielle; Geri Halliwell; Melanie C

Best British Single
Basement Jaxx - Red Alert; Supergrass - Moving; Moloko - Sing It Back; Blur - Tender; Robbie Williams - She's The One; Travis - Why Does It Always Rain On Me?; Fatboy Slim - Praise You; Manic Street Preachers - You Stole The Sun From My Heart; The Chemical Brothers - Hey Boy Hey Girl; Shanks And Bigfoot - Sweet Like Chocolate

Best British Video
Aphex Twin - Windowlicker; Fatboy Slim - Praise You; Robbie Williams - She's The One; The Chemical Brothers - Let Forever Be; Supergrass - Pumping On Your Stereo
