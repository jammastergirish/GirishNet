William Orbit's Solo Album

William Orbit has produced for Madonna, Blur, All Saints and Mel C, he is now going solo, Pieces in A Modern Style contains eleven classical pieces done in William Orbit's own ambient style. He plays everything himself and then samples it. Pieces in A Modern Style is released on the 17 January 2000, click here to pre-order the album from Amazon.co.uk.
